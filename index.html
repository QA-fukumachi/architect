04 — Artist

Biography

Architect is a Georgian DJ and producer operating at the intersection of raw, hypnotic techno and tightly controlled groove. His sound is defined by weight, tension, and precision built for immersive, high-intensity dancefloor experiences.

He first emerged with a split EP on Synergie, followed by the “Duplicity” EP, which gained support from respected international artists and positioned him within the underground circuit.

In 2025, Architect further refined his sonic identity with the “Resurrections” EP on Drawer Records and “Format X” EP on Differentsound two releases that marked a clear progression toward a more focused and mature sound.

Driven by powerful low-end structures, evolving textures, and relentless rhythmic flow, his productions reflect a disciplined and forward-thinking approach to techno. With a constantly evolving vision, Architect continues to carve his space in the underground, delivering uncompromising energy and depth on the dancefloor.
